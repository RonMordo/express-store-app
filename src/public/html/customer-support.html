1. Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Most orders are delivered within 3–7 business days. Express options are available at checkout for faster delivery.
2. Q: How can I track my order?
A: Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking link via email. You can also use our courier partners’ websites: UPS, DHL, or FedEx.
3. Q: What is your return policy?
A: You can return any product within 14 days of delivery, as long as it's unused and in its original packaging. Visit the Returns section in your profile to start a return.
4. Q: I received a damaged product. What should I do?
A: We're sorry! Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage, and we’ll help you with a replacement or refund.
5. Q: How do I contact customer support?
A: You can reach us at support@bitbasket.com or call +1 (555) 123-4567 during business hours (Mon–Fri, 9 AM–6 PM EST).
